Y2K SILVER NIKE TAG

Being known as a time fill of technological advancements, the 2000s involved plenty of aesthetics, one being a cyber, futuristic style. At the time, NIKE decided to change all of their labels and clothing tags from white to silver and grey. The full NIKE spellout was taken out and was replaced with a minimal grey swoosh on the outside areas of the tag. More information was added such as the sizing and production location along with a French version below.
