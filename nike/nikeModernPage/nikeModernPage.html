MODERN NIKE TAG

Nike tags today are very simple, offering a clean, sleek look with its use of only black and white. Unlike the 2010 labels, the modern tags use white as its background and black as the colour for its supporting elements. The logo stays the same with its combination of the swoosh and the Nike text. This tag can be seen on higher priced nike items, one being collabs between nike and another brand such as Stussy or Fear of God.
