CARHARTT PRESENT TAG

In 1966, Carhartt changed their logo to the Carhartt "C". Ever since then, Carhartt has used this logo to labal all of their garments. Their label consists of a 4:3 like rectangle with the logo aligned on the right and the Carhartt workmark below the logo. For the most part, information on the tags is unseen, usually displayed elsewhere.
