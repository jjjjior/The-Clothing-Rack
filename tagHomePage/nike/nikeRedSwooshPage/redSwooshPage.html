90s RED SWOOSH NIKE TAG

One signifier for vintage Nike tags were the colours used for its labels. Red became synonymous with Nike clothing labels during the 90s era. The red swoosh and black base became a transition from full logos that include the Nike lettermark to more minimalistic Nike tags that only include the swoosh.
