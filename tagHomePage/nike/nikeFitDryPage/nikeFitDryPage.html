NIKE FIT DRY TAG

Better known as "Dri-fit," Nike "fit dry" was another name for its type of sportswear technology. Just like the 90s and its correlation to the colour red, Nike labels in the 2000s used a lot of greytones, especially silver to build on the idea of a y2k aesthetic. However, Nike fit dry tags included a orange swoosh unlike regular y2k tags that only use silver and any other shade of grey. These tags are often associated with Nike golf and tennis wear.
