NIKE TEAM TAG

Nike Team tags are unlike your regular Nike clothing labels. The logo incorporates a white swoosh inside a red rectangle with the word "TEAM" across the bottom. The purpose of this unique tag was to label clothing items related to sports teams. Back in the late 90s and early 2000s, Nike had partial sponsorship over specific NBA teams, leading to jerseys and other sports related clothing pieces with the Nike team label attached to the back of the neck.
