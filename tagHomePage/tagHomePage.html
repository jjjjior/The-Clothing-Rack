My name is Jiordan Roque. I am a 2nd year graphic design student based in Toronto, Canada. Since around 4 years ago, thrifting became a passion. Watching youtubers such as Paul Cantu or Jacob Starr looking through the racks, finding crazy vintage pieces was a joy to watch. I started thrifting myself and over the past year of me going to my local Value Villages constantly, my knowledge of what to look for has expanded. One specific aspect to watch out for are clothing tags/labels. Notable brands such as Nike, Adidas, Champion, Levis, etc. all have a variety of tags that can date when that correlating clothing piece was made.
